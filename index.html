Why Learn with Qualdrive?

Approved Driving Instructors (ADI’s)

QualDrive Driving School has DVSA approved driving instructors with up-to-date knowledge and experience to ensure high standards are passed onto learners.

Online booking & secure payments!

QualDrive has an integrated online booking system for driving lessons which can be accessed anytime on your mobile devices or by logging in to your TotalDrive account. This makes the booking process easy, convenient, and flexible to suite your availability.

One to one instruction

Your dedicated instructor will guide and support you throughout the learning modules and remain your direct contact throughout the duration of your driver training.

Competitive prices

At QualDrive we ensure we remain competitively priced and have flexible lesson plans. Savings to be had with block bookings and gift vouchers available.

Dash cams (front & rear)

Your tuition vehicle is fitted with the latest dash cam software to aid with reflective practice and protects your safety.

Total drive Pupil and parent app

Electronic reflective logs, feedback & progress reports, lesson reminders, payments, and helpful learning resources all at your fingertips!
